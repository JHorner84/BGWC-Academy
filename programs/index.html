Programs

Two Programs.
One Academy.

Little Lions is the starting point. Youth Wrestling is the core. Both are built on the same BGWC Academy standard of discipline, character, and consistent development.

Foundational Youth Program
Little Lions

Little Lions Wrestling

Little Lions is the bridge into the BGWC Academy. It keeps the club identity strong while giving younger wrestlers an approachable place to start before they are ready for the intensity of the core youth program.

The focus is on building confidence, listening skills, coordination, and the basic habits of a wrestler. Athletes learn how to enter a room, how to warm up, how to follow instruction, and how to compete with respect.

  • Lower intensity entry point designed for younger athletes
  • Focus on confidence, coordination, and basic wrestling movement
  • Structure, listening, and accountability from day one
  • Transition pathway into the core Youth Wrestling program
  • Fun with purpose — every practice has a goal

Core Academy Program
Youth Wrestling

BGWC Youth Wrestling

This is the main program. Athletes in Youth Wrestling are held to a higher standard, trained with greater intensity, and developed with a clear eye on competition and long-term growth.

The coaching staff expects focus, effort, and respect every session. Athletes learn real technique, compete in USA Wrestling sanctioned events, and build the athletic and personal identity that defines a BGWC Academy wrestler.

  • Higher intensity training with real technical development
  • USA Wrestling sanctioned competition preparation
  • Mat discipline, mental toughness, and team accountability
  • Consistent practice structure with measurable athlete growth
  • Team culture, records, recognition, and competitive identity
