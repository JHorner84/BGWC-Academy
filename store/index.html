Store Options

Three ways to set up the store.

All three are free to start. Pick the one that fits how much work you want to put in.

🔥
Easiest

Bonfire

Upload your logo, design a tee or hoodie, get a shareable link. No inventory, no shipping headaches — Bonfire handles everything and sends you a check.

  • Free to set up, no monthly cost
  • No inventory or shipping to manage
  • Club gets a cut of every sale
  • Campaign-based — great for seasonal drops
Start on Bonfire
🖨️

Printful + Shopify

Full custom storefront with your branding. More product options, more control, slightly more setup. Shopify has a free trial; Printful is free until you sell.

  • Full branded store at your own URL
  • Dozens of product types available
  • Embeds directly on this page
  • Shopify has a 3-day free trial
Start on Printful
✏️

Custom Ink Fundraising

Designed specifically for clubs and teams. No minimums, fundraising-friendly, and families are used to ordering from them for school gear.

  • No order minimums
  • Built for school and youth clubs
  • Ships direct to each buyer
  • Fundraising-friendly pricing
Start on Custom Ink
