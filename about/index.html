Our Story

Nearly 20 years of building the right kind of athlete.

BGWC Academy Arizona was founded in 2005 with a simple but demanding idea: youth wrestling should develop the whole person. Not just the competitor. The athlete who leaves this program should carry something that lasts longer than any trophy.

We are a USA Wrestling chartered club based in Waddell, Arizona. Our two programs — Little Lions and Youth Wrestling — serve athletes at different stages of development but hold them to the same standard of effort, respect, and accountability.

This is not a rec program. It is not a drop-in class. BGWC Academy is a club with an identity, records, expectations, and a room that takes itself seriously.

The Standard

What we expect. What you get.

Every family joining BGWC Academy should understand what the room requires and what the room delivers.

01

Show Up Ready

Athletes are expected to be on time, in proper gear, and ready to work from the moment practice begins. Consistency in the room builds consistency on the mat.

02

Respect the Room

Coaches, teammates, opponents, and the facility are all treated with respect. The BGWC Academy standard of conduct applies during practice, tournaments, and travel.

03

Keep Growing

Athletes are not expected to be perfect. They are expected to put in the effort, receive coaching well, and track their own development seriously over time.

04

Parents Support the Room

The coaching staff communicates clearly and expects families to follow the communication policy, stay off the mat, and let the coaches coach.

05

Clear Expectations

There are no vague rules here. Athletes and parents know the code of conduct, the visitor policy, the communication guidelines, and what happens when they are not followed.

06

Team Identity

You are representing BGWC Academy Arizona. That means something. Athletes carry the crest at tournaments, on the mat, and in how they treat others.

USA Wrestling

A Properly Chartered Club

BGWC Academy Arizona is a fully chartered USA Wrestling club. That means our athletes compete under a recognized charter, our coaches meet USA Wrestling standards, and families can trust that the program is organized and legitimate.

USA Wrestling membership gives athletes access to sanctioned tournaments, insurance coverage during club activities, and a pathway into the broader wrestling development system in Arizona and nationally.
