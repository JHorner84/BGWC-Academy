Arizona Tax Credit

Your donation could cost you nothing.

Arizona allows qualified charitable contributions to certain organizations as a dollar-for-dollar state income tax credit. Talk to your tax advisor to confirm eligibility.

If BGWC Academy Arizona qualifies under the Arizona Charitable Tax Credit (or Extracurricular Activity Credit), Arizona residents who donate may be able to claim the full amount as a credit against their state income taxes — not just a deduction, a full credit.

This means a $200 donation could reduce your Arizona tax bill by $200. For families already paying state taxes, this is effectively a cost-free way to support the club.

Consult your tax professional to confirm eligibility and the correct form to use. BGWC Academy can provide documentation of your contribution.
