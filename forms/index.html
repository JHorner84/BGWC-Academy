Google Workspace for BGWC Academy

One place for everything.

All club forms, submissions, rosters, and records run through Google's free suite. Here is how the system works and what each tool does for you.

📋

Google Forms

Build registration, waiver, inquiry, and contact forms for free. All submissions are captured automatically with no monthly cost.

📊

Google Sheets

Every form submission flows into a spreadsheet automatically. You see athlete rosters, waiver status, and contact lists in real time.

📁

Google Drive

Store practice schedules, tournament rosters, club documents, and coach resources all in one shared folder your staff can access.

📧

Gmail

Set up a club email like [email protected] or use a custom domain (bgwcacademy.com) for a professional address.

📅

Google Calendar

Publish a shared practice and tournament calendar families can subscribe to. Updates push automatically to every subscriber.

🔔

Form Notifications

Google Forms sends you an email every time a family submits. You can also set up conditional notifications for specific programs.

Setup Guide

How to replace the placeholders above

  1. Go to forms.google.com and sign in with your club Gmail account.
  2. Create a new form for each tab: Registration, Waivers, Inquiry, and Contact. Build your questions, set response destination to Google Sheets.
  3. Get the embed code: Click Send → click the <> embed icon → copy the iframe HTML.
  4. Open the forms/index.html file in a text editor and find the placeholder comments. Replace the entire <div class="gform-placeholder">...</div> block with your copied iframe.
  5. Save, commit, and push to GitHub. The live site updates within a minute.
