Club Standards

Rules protect the room.

These are not suggestions. They apply to every athlete, parent, and visitor who enters the BGWC Academy program.

Athlete Code of Conduct

Add the specific code of conduct for athletes here. Cover behavior during practice, tournaments, and travel. Be specific about what is expected and what the consequences are for violations.

Parent Expectations

Add parent expectations here. Cover sideline behavior, communication with coaches, how and when to raise concerns, and what is not acceptable during practice or competition.

Communication Policy

Add the communication policy here. Specify how parents should contact coaches, response time expectations, and what channels are not appropriate for club business.

Visitor Policy

Add the visitor policy here. Who can attend practice, where they should stand, what they cannot do, and the process for first-time visitors or prospective families.

Equipment Standards

Add gear requirements here. What athletes are expected to bring, what is provided, and what is required before the first practice. Wrestling shoes, singlet requirements, headgear rules.

Safety & Medical Policy

Add the club's health and safety protocols here. Illness policy, injury reporting, return-to-participation guidelines, and concussion protocol consistent with USA Wrestling standards.
