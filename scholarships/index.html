Scholarships & Sponsorship

No Kid Gets
Left Behind.

Financial hardship should never be the reason an athlete does not compete. BGWC Academy Arizona offers annual scholarships, sliding scale dues, and a Sponsor-a-Wrestler program so every deserving kid gets on the mat.

"No kid should be prevented from becoming a champion because they are less fortunate."

Annual Scholarships

Two full scholarships. Every year.

Each season BGWC Academy Arizona awards two full scholarships to athletes who demonstrate need and commitment. These are not charity — they are an investment in athletes who have earned their place in the room.

01
Annual Scholarship

Full Year Coverage

One athlete receives full 12-month dues coverage — no financial barrier to participation. Selected by the coaching staff based on demonstrated need and commitment to the program.

  • Full 12-month dues coverage
  • All program fees included
  • Athlete must remain in good standing
  • Renewable based on continued need and participation

Sliding Scale

We will work with every family.

Beyond the two annual scholarships, BGWC Academy operates on a sliding scale for families who need it. If standard dues create a hardship, talk to the coaching staff. We will find a number that works.

This is not widely advertised because it is not a program — it is just how this club operates. If dues are a stretch, reach out privately. The conversation stays between you and the coaching staff.

Why sponsor?

Wrestling builds discipline, character, and resilience in ways most youth sports do not. The athletes in this program are being shaped into people who contribute to this community. Your sponsorship is an investment in that outcome.

You choose whether to sponsor a specific athlete or leave the selection to the coaching staff. You choose whether your name appears on the sponsor wall or stays private. All we ask is that you care about what happens to kids in this community.
