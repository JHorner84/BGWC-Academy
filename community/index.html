About Band

Why the club uses Band.

Band is a free group communication app built specifically for teams and clubs. It keeps club communication separate from personal texts and social media — which means it is easier to manage, easier to search, and easier for coaches to reach everyone at once.

When the coaching staff posts a practice change or tournament update, every member of the group gets notified immediately. No text chains, no missed emails, no confusion.

📢
Instant announcements Coaches post once, everyone in the group gets notified right away
📅
Event scheduling Tournaments, practices, and club events posted with RSVP tracking
📸
Photos and media Practice and tournament photos shared directly in the group feed
🔒
Private and invite-only Only club members with the link can join — no public exposure
📱
Free app, iOS and Android Download once, use it all season — no subscription required

Getting Started

Four steps to join the group.

Takes about two minutes. Every family with an athlete in the program should complete these steps.

1

Download Band

Get the free Band app from the App Store or Google Play on your phone.

2

Create an Account

Sign up with your email or phone number. Use your real name so the coaching staff recognizes you.

3

Use the Invite Link

Tap the club invite link below. It takes you directly into the BGWC Academy Arizona group.

4

Turn On Notifications

Allow notifications from Band so you never miss a practice change or tournament update.

Group Standards

How we use the group.

The Band group is for club business. The same standards that apply in the room apply in the group.

Club Info Only

The group is for practice updates, tournament info, schedules, and club announcements. It is not a general chat or social feed.

Respect the Room

The BGWC Academy code of conduct applies to the group. Negative comments about athletes, coaches, or other families are not permitted.

Coach Communication

For questions or concerns directed at the coaching staff, follow the communication policy. The group is not the place for individual issues.
